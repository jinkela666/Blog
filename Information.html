About us



"Protecting the environment is a common obejctive, not personal."

Our mission is to raise awareness about the heavy environmental pollution that is happening all around the world. We think that the key to achieving a pollutant free world is by letting everyone know about what the Earth is suffering and the destructive effect on people's life. In the 21st century, with the rise of Internet, information is circulating faster than ever. Blogs and social networks are starting to be popular among teenagers and young adults, for this reason we thought that the blog was a good way to promote information about the environment and the way to protect it.

"Protecting the environment is protecting ourself."
